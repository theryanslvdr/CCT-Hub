# CrossCurrent Hub - Member Guide
## What's New: Step-by-Step Instructions for Every Enhancement

> This guide walks you through every new feature added to CrossCurrent Hub.
> Follow the steps exactly as written. Screenshots of each feature are included below.

## How to Navigate the New Menu

The sidebar menu has been completely redesigned. Instead of one big list, everything is now organized into **categories** that you can click to expand or collapse.

### Step-by-step:
1. **Log in** to CrossCurrent Hub
2. Look at the **left sidebar**
3. You'll see these categories:
   - **CORE** - Your main trading tools
   - **GROWTH** - Team building & habits
   - **REWARDS** - Your points and achievements
   - **COMMUNITY** - Forum, AI help, and booking calls
   - **TOOLS** - Hub Store
4. **Click on a category name** to expand it and see the items inside
5. **Click again** to collapse it
6. The menu **automatically opens** the right category when you navigate to a page

**What you'll see:**

```
CORE          >     (click to expand)
  Dashboard
  Profit Tracker
  Trade Monitor

GROWTH        >     (click to expand)
  Daily Habits
  Affiliate Center
  My Team

REWARDS       >     (click to expand)
  My Rewards

COMMUNITY     >     (click to expand)
  Community Forum
  AI Assistant
  Book a Call        <-- NEW!

TOOLS         >     (click to expand)
  Hub Store          <-- renamed from "Store"
```

## Feature 1: Book a Call (NEW)

You can now schedule a call directly inside CrossCurrent Hub.

### Step-by-step:
1. In the sidebar, **click on COMMUNITY** to expand it
2. Click **"Book a Call"**
3. You'll see a booking calendar embedded in the page
4. **Choose a date and time** that works for you
5. **Fill in your details** and confirm the booking
6. Done! You'll receive a confirmation

> **Note:** If you see "Booking calendar not configured yet", it means your admin hasn't set up the booking link yet. Ask your team leader to configure it.

---

## Feature 2: Quick Action Buttons on Dashboard (NEW)

Your Dashboard now has quick shortcut buttons to jump to common tasks.

### Step-by-step:
1. Go to your **Dashboard** (click Dashboard in the sidebar)
2. **Scroll down** past the stat cards and performance chart
3. You'll see a row of **Quick Action** buttons:
   - **Track Profits** - Jump straight to the Profit Tracker
   - **Daily Habits** - Go to your habit checklist
   - **View Rewards** - Check your points and badges
   - **Manage Team** - Go to your team page (only visible if you have team members)
4. **Click any button** to jump to that page instantly

---

## Feature 3: Hub Store (Renamed + New Items)

The "Store" has been renamed to **"Hub Store"** and now includes Streak Freezes.

### Step-by-step:
1. In the sidebar, **click on TOOLS** to expand it
2. Click **"Hub Store"**
3. You'll see items available for purchase:
   - **Signal Gate Immunity** - Protects you from signal gate restrictions
   - **Streak Freeze** - Saves your daily streak if you miss a day (this used to be in My Rewards)
4. Check your **point balance** at the top
5. Click **"Purchase"** on any item to buy it with your earned points

---

## Feature 4: Leaderboard (Now in My Rewards)

The Leaderboard has been moved inside the My Rewards page.

### Step-by-step:
1. In the sidebar, **click on REWARDS** to expand it
2. Click **"My Rewards"**
3. Look for the **"Leaderboard"** button on the page
4. **Click it** to open the leaderboard popup
5. You'll see the **community rankings** - who has the most points, longest streaks, etc.
6. **Click outside** the popup or press X to close it

---

## Feature 5: Invite & Earn (Now on My Team Page)

The "Invite & Earn" referral card has been moved to the top of the My Team page.

### Step-by-step:
1. In the sidebar, **click on GROWTH** to expand it
2. Click **"My Team"**
3. At the very top of the page, you'll see the **"Invite & Earn"** card
4. It shows:
   - Your personal **referral code** (highlighted in orange)
   - Your **invite link** - copy this and share it
   - A **"Copy"** button to quickly copy the link
   - Your **direct signup link** for Merin Global Trading
5. **Click "Copy"** and share the link with people you want to invite

---

## Feature 6: Weekly Performance Report (NEW - Team Leaders)

If you have team members, you'll now see a **Weekly Performance Report** on your My Team page.

### Step-by-step:
1. In the sidebar, **click on GROWTH** > **"My Team"**
2. Below the stat cards (Total Members, Active This Week, etc.), look for the **"Weekly Performance Report"** section
3. This shows your team's performance **for the current week**:
   - **Total Trades** - how many trades your team made this week
   - **Total Profit** - combined profit/loss in dollars
   - **Win Rate** - what percentage of trades were winners
   - **Active Traders** - how many team members are actively trading (out of total)
4. Each metric shows a **comparison to last week** with a green arrow (up) or red arrow (down)
5. If someone on your team is doing great, you'll see a **"Top Performer"** highlight with their name and stats
6. Below all that is a **Member Breakdown** table showing each person's individual stats

> **Note:** If you don't have any team members yet, you won't see this section. Use the Invite & Earn link above to grow your team!

## Feature 7: AI Assistant Can Post to Forum (NEW)

When the AI Assistant doesn't know the answer to your question, it can now create a forum post for the community to help.

### Step-by-step:
1. In the sidebar, **click on COMMUNITY** > **"AI Assistant"**
2. Type a question in the chat box and press send
3. If the AI **can answer**, it will respond as usual
4. If the AI **cannot answer**, you'll see a button that says **"Post to Forum"**
5. Click **"Post to Forum"** to automatically create a new forum topic with your question
6. Other community members can then see your question and help answer it

---

## Feature 8: Enhanced Dashboard Performance View

Your Dashboard now shows more detailed trading performance metrics.

### Step-by-step:
1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Look at the **stat cards** at the top:
   - **Account Value** - your current account balance
   - **Total Profit** - lifetime profit with percentage vs projected
   - **Total Trades** - number of trades logged
   - **Actual vs Projected** - how your actual results compare to projections
3. The **Performance Overview** chart shows your actual vs projected performance over time
4. On the right side, you'll see the **"No Trade Members"** panel showing who hasn't traded today

---

## Feature 9: Share Your Performance

You can share your trading performance with a nice visual card.

### Step-by-step:
1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click the **"Share"** button in the top right corner
3. A popup will appear showing your performance stats as a shareable card
4. It displays your **streak**, **profit**, and **trade count**
5. Copy or share this card to show your progress

## Using CrossCurrent on Mobile

The entire platform now works on your phone!

### Step-by-step:
1. Open CrossCurrent Hub on your **mobile browser**
2. The sidebar is hidden - instead you'll see a **hamburger menu** icon at the top
3. Tap it to open the **full-screen navigation menu**
4. At the **bottom of the screen**, you'll see a navigation bar with 5 quick tabs:
   - **Home** - Dashboard
   - **Tracker** - Profit Tracker
   - **Trade** - Log a new trade
   - **Heartbeat** - Check your heartbeat status
   - **Rewards** - View your rewards
5. All pages are fully responsive - tables scroll horizontally, cards stack vertically
6. The **Book a Call** page works great on mobile - the calendar fills the full screen
