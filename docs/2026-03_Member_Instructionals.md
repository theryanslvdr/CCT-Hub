## Your First Day on CrossCurrent Hub

Welcome! Here's how to get started in your first session.

### Step 1: Log In
1. Open your browser and go to the Hub URL provided by your admin
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. If prompted to change your password, set a new secure password

### Step 2: Explore Your Dashboard
Once logged in, you'll land on the **Dashboard**:
- **Account Value** — Your current trading account balance
- **Total Profit** — How much you've earned so far
- **Rewards Card** — Your points, level, and monthly rank

### Step 3: Check the Daily Signal
Look for the **Today's Trading Signal** card. It will show:
- The product to trade (e.g., MOIL10)
- Direction (BUY or SELL)
- The time to execute

### Step 4: Navigate Using the Sidebar
On desktop, use the left sidebar to access:
- **Dashboard** — Overview of your finances
- **Profit Tracker** — Detailed projections and history
- **Trade Monitor** — Execute and record trades
- **My Rewards** — Points, badges, and streak freezes
- **Leaderboard** — See your ranking among members
- **Community Forum** — Ask questions, share knowledge
- **Daily Habits** — Track your daily routine
- **Affiliate Center** — Your referral link and stats

## How to Execute & Record Your Daily Trade

This is your most important daily task on the Hub.

### Step 1: Check the Signal
1. Go to **Profit Tracker** or **Dashboard**
2. Look at **Today's Trading Signal**
3. Note the product, direction (BUY/SELL), and time

### Step 2: Execute the Trade on Your Trading Platform
- Open your trading platform (e.g., Merin)
- At the signal time, place your trade using the correct product and direction
- Use your LOT size (shown on the Hub)
- Close the trade when the target is reached

### Step 3: Record the Trade on the Hub
1. Go to **Trade Monitor**
2. In the **Trade Control** section:
   - Enter your **Entry** price
   - Enter your **Exit** price
3. The system calculates your profit automatically
4. Click **Submit Trade**

### Step 4: Verify
- Your trade appears in **Trade History**
- Your **streak counter** updates (if profitable)
- Points are automatically awarded for eligible actions

### Today's Signal Section

## Reading the Monthly Projection Table

The monthly table shows your complete daily trading record and projections.

### Column Guide
| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Date** | The trading day (weekdays only, holidays excluded) |
| **Balance Before** | Your account balance at the **start** of that day (before any trades or transactions) |
| **LOT Size** | The lot size calculated from your Balance Before |
| **Target Profit** | What you should earn: LOT Size x 15 |
| **Actual Profit** | What you actually earned (blank if no trade yet) |
| **Commission** | Commission received from the trade |
| **Status** | Check mark = trade recorded, Calendar = pending/future |

### How to Read It
1. **Past days** (with trades): Shows your actual results vs. target
2. **Today**: Highlighted row showing today's target
3. **Future days**: Projected values based on your current balance

### Important Notes
- **Balance Before** updates after deposits and withdrawals
- If you withdrew money on Monday, Tuesday's Balance Before will be lower
- The LOT size and target adjust automatically based on your balance

## How to Make a Deposit

### Step 1: Initiate Deposit
1. Go to **Dashboard** or **Transactions** section
2. Click the **Deposit** button
3. Select your payment method

### Step 2: Complete Payment
1. Enter the deposit amount
2. Follow the payment instructions
3. Upload payment proof if required

### Step 3: Wait for Approval

## How to Request a Withdrawal

### Step 1: Submit Request
1. Go to the **Transactions** section
2. Click **Withdraw**
3. Enter the amount you want to withdraw (must not exceed your balance)
4. Provide your wallet address

### Step 2: Confirm Details
- Double-check the amount and wallet address
- Review the remaining balance preview
- Click **Submit**

### Step 3: Wait for Processing
- Admin reviews and processes your withdrawal
- Your balance is reduced when **approved** (not when requested)
- You'll be notified when complete

### Important
- Withdrawals affect your next trading day's Balance Before
- If you withdraw after a trade on Monday, Tuesday's balance will reflect the withdrawal
- Your LOT size and daily target adjust accordingly

## Hiding Your Financial Values (Privacy Mode)

Each card in the Profit Tracker has its own privacy toggle.

### How to Hide Values
1. Go to **Profit Tracker**
2. Look for the **eye icon** on any summary card
3. Click the eye icon to toggle:

- Deposits
- Total Profit

## How to Buy & Use Streak Freezes

Streak freezes protect your trading and habit streaks if you miss a day.

### Step 1: Go to Streak Freezes Section
1. Navigate to **My Rewards**
2. Scroll to the **Streak Freezes** section

### Step 2: Purchase Freezes
1. Choose the type:
   - **Trade Streak Freeze**: 200 points — protects your trading streak
   - **Habit Streak Freeze**: 150 points — protects your habit streak
2. Select quantity (1-10)
3. Review total cost
4. Click **Buy**
5. Points are deducted, freezes added to your inventory

### Step 3: How They Activate
- Freezes are **automatic** — no action needed from you
- If you miss a trading day (or habit day), the system checks your inventory
- If you have a freeze, it's consumed and your streak continues
- If you don't have a freeze, your streak resets to 0

### Tips
- Keep at least 1-2 freezes in inventory at all times
- Buy in bulk when you have extra points
- Check your usage history at the bottom of the section

## Understanding LOT Size & Daily Target

### What Is LOT Size?
Your LOT size determines how much you trade. It's calculated from your account balance:

```
LOT Size = truncate(Account Value / 980, 2 decimals)
```

**Important:** This uses **truncation** (floors down), not rounding.

### Example Calculation
| Account Value | Calculation | LOT Size |
|---------------|-------------|----------|
| $10,000 | 10,000 / 980 = 10.204... | **10.20** |
| $25,000 | 25,000 / 980 = 25.510... | **25.51** |
| $50,000 | 50,000 / 980 = 51.020... | **51.02** |

### Daily Target
```
Daily Target = LOT Size x 15
```

| LOT Size | Daily Target |
|----------|-------------|
| 10.20 | $153.00 |
| 25.51 | $382.65 |
| 51.02 | $765.30 |

### When Does LOT Size Change?
- When your account balance changes (deposits, withdrawals, profits)
- The LOT size is **fixed for the entire quarter** (recalculated at quarter start)
- Quarterly dates: Jan 1, Apr 1, Jul 1, Oct 1

## Understanding Streaks & How They Work

### What Is a Streak?
A streak counts how many consecutive **trading days** you've recorded a trade.

### Trading Days
- **Counts**: Monday through Friday (weekdays)
- **Doesn't count**: Saturdays, Sundays, US market holidays
- Missing a weekend or holiday does **NOT** break your streak

### How Streaks Break
Your streak resets to 0 if you miss a weekday that isn't a holiday, **unless** you have a streak freeze.

### Streak Rewards
| Streak Milestone | Points |
|-----------------|--------|
| Every 5 days | 50 points (repeatable!) |

### Streak Badges
| Badge | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| Streak Starter | 3 consecutive days |
| Streak Master 7 | 7 consecutive days |
| Streak Master 14 | 14 consecutive days |
| Streak Master 30 | 30 consecutive days |
| Streak Champion | 50 consecutive days |
| Streak Legend | 100 consecutive days |

### Protecting Your Streak
1. Go to **My Rewards** > **Streak Freezes**
2. Buy freezes (200 pts for Trade, 150 pts for Habit)
3. They auto-activate when you miss a day
4. Keep 1-2 in stock for emergencies
