## Account & Login FAQs

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
**A:** 
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your registered email
4. Check your inbox (and spam folder)
5. Click the reset link (valid for 1 hour)
6. Enter your new password

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, contact an admin.

### Q: How do I change my password?
**A:**
1. Click your name in the sidebar
2. Go to "Profile"
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter new password (twice to confirm)
6. Click "Save"

### Q: How do I update my profile information?
**A:**
1. Go to Profile (click your name or use dropdown)
2. Edit your Full Name if needed
3. Update your email (may require verification)
4. Save changes

### Q: Why does my session keep logging out?
**A:** Sessions expire after a period of inactivity for security. To stay logged in longer:
- Enable "Remember Me" on login (if available)
- Keep the tab active
- Avoid using incognito/private browsing

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?
**A:** No, each person should have one account. Multiple accounts may be flagged and suspended.

## Trading FAQs

### Q: Where do I find today's trading signal?
**A:** The daily signal appears in multiple places:
1. **Dashboard** - Signal card on main page
2. **Profit Tracker** - "Today's Trading Signal" section
3. **Trade Monitor** - Top of the page

Signals typically include: Product code, Direction (BUY/SELL), Time

### Q: How is my LOT size calculated?
**A:** 
```
LOT Size = truncate(Account Value ÷ 980, 2 decimals)
```

**Important:** Uses truncation (floor), NOT rounding.

Example:
- Account Value: $30,000
- Calculation: 30,000 ÷ 980 = 30.612...
- LOT Size: **30.61** (truncated to 2 decimals)

### Q: What should my daily profit target be?
**A:**
```
Daily Target = LOT Size × 15
```

Example:
- LOT Size: 30.61
- Target: 30.61 × 15 = **$459.15**

### Q: How do I record a trade?
**A:**
1. Go to **Trade Monitor**
2. Enter your **Entry** price
3. Enter your **Exit** price
4. Verify the calculated profit
5. Click **"Submit Trade"**
6. Trade appears in history

### Q: Can I edit a past trade?
**A:** Yes, if you made an error:
1. Go to Trade Monitor → History
2. Find the trade
3. Click the edit icon
4. Update Entry/Exit prices
5. Save changes

Note: Editing affects your profit calculations retroactively.

### Q: What counts as a "trading day"?
**A:** Trading days are weekdays (Monday-Friday) excluding US market holidays. Weekends and holidays don't count for:
- Streak calculations
- Daily projections
- Trading targets

### Q: Why did my streak reset?
**A:** Possible reasons:
1. Missed a trading day (weekday that wasn't a holiday)
2. Streak freeze not available
3. System error (contact support)

Check your streak status in Trade Monitor or My Rewards.

## Profit Tracker FAQs

### Q: What does "Balance Before" mean?
**A:** The **Balance Before** column shows your account value at the **start** of that trading day, before any trades, deposits, or withdrawals that day.

### Q: Why doesn't my "Balance Before" match my actual balance?
**A:** Common reasons:
1. **Pending sync** - Click "Sync" button on Account Value card
2. **Withdrawal not processed** - Check transaction status
3. **Deposit in progress** - Wait for admin approval
4. **Calculation delay** - Refresh the page

If still wrong, contact admin with specific dates.

### Q: How do I hide my financial values?
**A:** Each summary card has an **eye icon** (👁):
- Click to hide → Shows "••••••"
- Click again to show
- Each card toggles independently

### Q: What is the "Sync" button for?
**A:** The Sync button verifies your account balance with the Merin trading platform:
1. Click "Sync" on Account Value card
2. Enter your Merin balance
3. System compares values
4. Alerts if discrepancy found

Use this if you suspect calculation errors.

### Q: How do I export my trading data?
**A:**
1. Go to Profit Tracker
2. Navigate to Monthly Table
3. Click "Export" button
4. CSV file downloads with all data

1. Click "Open Rewards & Store" on My Rewards page

### Q: Why didn't I get my badge?
**A:** Badges are awarded automatically when you meet criteria. If missing:
1. Verify you meet the requirement
2. Wait a few minutes (processing delay)

## Forum FAQs

### Q: How do I ask a question?
**A:**
1. Go to Community Forum
2. Click "New Post"
3. Write clear title (what you need help with)
4. Describe problem in detail
5. Add relevant tags
6. Optionally attach screenshots
7. Click "Post"

### Q: How do I attach images to posts/comments?
**A:**
1. Look for "Attach Images" section
2. Click "Add Image" button
3. Select file (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP)
4. Max 2MB per image, up to 4 images
5. Preview shows before posting

### Q: How do I mark a post as solved?
**A:** If you created the post:
1. Open your post
2. Find the best answer comment
3. Click "Mark as Best Answer"
4. Optionally select collaborators
5. Click "Close Post"

### Q: What happens when I upvote/downvote?
**A:**
- **Upvote** (👍): Shows comment is helpful, adds to author's reputation
- **Downvote** (👎): Shows comment isn't helpful
- Votes are visible (you can see who voted)
- Authors earn leaderboard credit from upvotes

### Q: How is reputation calculated?
**A:**
```
Reputation = (10 × best_answers) + upvotes_received + (0.5 × comments_count)
```

Higher reputation = higher on Top Contributors list.

## Deposits & Withdrawals FAQs

### Q: How do I make a deposit?
**A:**
1. Go to your Dashboard or Transactions
2. Click "Deposit" button
3. Select payment method
4. Enter amount
5. Complete payment
6. Upload proof (if required)
7. Wait for admin approval

### Q: How long do deposits take to process?
**A:** Processing time depends on:
- Payment method verification
- Admin availability
- Typically 1-24 hours

### Q: How do I withdraw funds?
**A:**
1. Go to Transactions
2. Click "Withdraw"
3. Enter amount (must be ≤ available balance)
4. Provide wallet address
5. Submit request
6. Admin reviews and processes

### Q: Why was my withdrawal rejected?
**A:** Possible reasons:
- Insufficient balance
- Invalid wallet address
- Pending verification required
- Contact admin for specific reason

### Q: When do withdrawals reflect in my balance?
**A:** Your balance is reduced when:
- Withdrawal is **approved** (not when requested)
- This prevents double-spending

### Q: Is there a minimum/maximum withdrawal?
**A:** Check with admin for current limits. Typically:
- Minimum: $50
- Maximum: Based on available balance

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Page won't load / shows blank
**Try:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try incognito/private window
4. Try different browser
5. Check internet connection

### Problem: Can't log in
**Try:**
1. Verify email is correct
2. Check caps lock
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cookies
5. Contact admin if persistent

### Problem: Trade not showing in history
**Try:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Wait 1-2 minutes for processing
3. Check if you clicked "Submit Trade"
4. Verify date filter isn't hiding it

### Problem: Points not awarded
**Check:**
1. Did you meet all requirements?
2. Is it an auto-award or manual claim?
3. Wait a few minutes for processing
4. Refresh the page
5. Contact admin with details

### Problem: Images won't upload in forum
**Check:**
1. File size (must be <2MB)
2. File type (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP only)
3. Wait for upload to complete
4. Check internet connection
5. Try different image

### Problem: Mobile display issues
**Try:**
1. Rotate device (try landscape/portrait)
2. Use mobile browser menu
3. Clear mobile browser cache
4. Update your browser
5. Try desktop site option
